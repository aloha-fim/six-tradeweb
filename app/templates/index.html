3) THIS IS A NEW SIX PRODUCT FEATURE FOR EACH BOND PRODUCT - OTHER SIX CLIENT MARKS VS TRADEWEB AI-PRICE

"Tradeweb Ai-Price tells you what a bond is worth. SIX CLIENT consensus tells you whether the people actually holding it agree — and on bonds that don't trade, that's the only second opinion that exists."

* Genuinely unique / a moat — Tradeweb can't compute it (it doesn't see client books), and no single bank can (it only sees its own mark). Only SIX as the distributor-in-the-middle can.

SIX Consensus deviation to Tradeweb Ai-Price vs SIX client bank marks. For each bond, how far Ai-Price sits from where the five bank holders carry it, expressed as a z-score against their dispersion. 6 of 12 screened come back off-market (the +1670 and +17.71 entries). This is the high-value signal — Tradeweb literally cannot compute it because it never sees client books. What crosses is the deviation magnitude and the off-market flag, not the marks themselves.
